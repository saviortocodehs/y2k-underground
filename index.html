This is my little corner to document weird, beautiful, occasionally broken music from people who probably recorded half of it in bedrooms and cracked DAWs. No algorithms, no playlists chasing trends — just scenes, friends, and things I love.

If you make stuff that feels like dial-up dreams, scratched CDs, crowded basements, or soft synth angels, you belong here.
